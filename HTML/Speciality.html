Department of Plastic & Cosmetic Surgery

The Department of Plastic & Cosmetic Surgery offers a wide range of services including reconstructive surgery, cosmetic enhancements, and treatment for burns and injuries. Our experienced surgeons utilize advanced techniques to provide optimal outcomes for patients.

Nephrology with Dialysis Facility

The Nephrology Department provides comprehensive care for patients with kidney diseases. We offer state-of-the-art dialysis facilities and specialized treatment for various renal conditions, ensuring the best possible care and management for our patients.

Department of Gastroenterology

The Department of Gastroenterology specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of digestive disorders. Our team of experts offers advanced procedures and therapies for conditions such as ulcers, liver diseases, and gastrointestinal cancers.

Department of Dentistry

The Department of Dentistry provides comprehensive dental care services including preventive, restorative, and cosmetic treatments. Our experienced dentists offer personalized care to address each patient's unique needs and concerns.

Department of Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The Department of Obstetrics & Gynaecology offers specialized care for women's health issues including prenatal care, childbirth, and gynecological conditions. Our dedicated team provides compassionate care to support women throughout every stage of life.

Department of Radiology

The Department of Radiology provides advanced imaging services including X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasound. Our experienced radiologists utilize state-of-the-art technology to accurately diagnose and monitor various medical conditions.

Department of Psychiatry

The Department of Psychiatry offers comprehensive mental health services including assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of various psychiatric disorders. Our team of psychiatrists and therapists provide personalized care to support patients' mental well-being.

Department of ENT

The Department of Otorhinolaryngology (ENT) specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the ear, nose, and throat. Our ENT specialists offer a wide range of services including surgical and non-surgical treatments for ENT conditions.

Department of Cardiology

The Department of Cardiology provides comprehensive cardiac care services including diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart-related conditions. Our team of cardiologists offers state-of-the-art interventions to improve heart health and quality of life.

PICU & NICU

The Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU) and Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) provide specialized care for critically ill infants and children. Our dedicated team of pediatric specialists offers round-the-clock monitoring and support to ensure the best possible outcomes for young patients.

Orthopaedics and Joint Replacement

The Department of Orthopaedics specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal disorders including fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries. Our orthopedic surgeons are experts in joint replacement surgeries, offering personalized care to restore mobility and function.

Department of Neurosurgery

The Department of Neurosurgery offers advanced surgical treatment for disorders of the nervous system including brain and spinal cord conditions. Our neurosurgeons utilize state-of-the-art techniques to provide optimal outcomes for patients with complex neurological conditions.

Kidney Transplant

The Kidney Transplant Program offers life-saving kidney transplantation for patients with end-stage renal disease. Our multidisciplinary team provides comprehensive pre-transplant evaluation, surgical expertise, and post-transplant care to ensure successful outcomes for transplant recipients.
